Let's learn making Butter

ButterPaneer is a delicious Indian dish made with paneer (cottage cheese) and butter.

Here are the ingredients you'll need:

  • 200g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cream

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat butter and add chopped onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute. Then add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  3. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add the paneer cubes and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Add garam masala and cream. Stir gently and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Enjoy your creamy Butter Paneer!
