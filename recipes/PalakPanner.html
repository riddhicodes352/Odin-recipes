Let's learn making Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a popular Indian dish made with fresh spinach and paneer (cottage cheese).

Here are the ingredients you'll need:

  • 200g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Blanch the spinach leaves in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then transfer them to cold water. Drain and blend into a smooth puree.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute. Then add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the spinach puree and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  6. Add the paneer cubes and garam masala. Stir gently and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with roti or rice.

Enjoy your delicious Palak Paneer!
